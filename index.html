Technology Guardians Rwanda (TGR)



  • Tech Guardians Rwanda is dedicated to Empowering Rwandan youth to leverage Technology Responsibly while addressing its negative impacts among young minds. This initiative strives to create a paradigm shift in Technology use by educating young individuals and communities, Creating advocacy about how they can effectively utilize Technology, advocating for mental well-being, and fostering Responsible tech engagement to boost productivity.
